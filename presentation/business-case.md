# AI Compliance Scanner - Business Case & ROI

## Market Opportunity

### Total Addressable Market
- **Policy Management**: $2.1B market (2024)
- **Compliance Automation**: $1.8B segment
- **Organization-Specific Security**: $900M (45% annual growth)
- **Target Customers**: Organizations with custom policies and compliance requirements

### Market Pain Points
- **Generic Rules**: Tools analyze against vendor rules, not your organization's policies
